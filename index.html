Biography of MSDhoni

Biography

Indian professional cricket player MS Dhoni is a right-handed hitter and wicketkeeper.

Skills

Dhoni is renowned for his composed temperament, superb judgment, and capacity to guide his team to success.

Experience

As one of the most successful captains in Indian cricket history, Dhoni has led the team in the majority of international matches

Education

Through the sports quota, Dhoni was admitted to Gossener College, Ranchi in 1999 to pursue a Bachelor of Commerce (BCom).
